Who We Are

Built for modern household management

Project FAB is a home automation and coordination service designed to reduce the administrative overhead of running a household. We build and operate smart communication tools that keep families informed, organised, and connected — across the platforms they already use every day.

Our services are delivered on an opt-in basis to household members and authorised contacts only. We take privacy seriously and never engage with individuals who have not explicitly consented to receive communications from our systems.
